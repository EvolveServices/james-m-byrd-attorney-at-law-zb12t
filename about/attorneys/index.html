Criminal Defense Attorney

James M. Byrd

Attorney at Law • Mobile, Alabama

James M. Byrd is a lifelong Mobile, Alabama resident and one of the most experienced criminal defense attorneys in the state. Born and raised in Mobile, Mr. Byrd graduated from Jefferson Davis High School in Montgomery before pursuing higher education at Duke University. He earned his undergraduate degree in Political Science and History from The University of Alabama, where he developed the analytical foundation that would define his legal career.

Inspired by a family tradition of criminal defense law, Mr. Byrd pursued his calling to The University of Alabama School of Law. In 1979 he earned his Juris Doctor and passed the Alabama State Bar. He returned immediately to Mobile and joined a team of experienced criminal defense attorneys, immersing himself in the practice he was born to do.

Since October 1979, James M. Byrd has practiced criminal defense every single day, without exception. His practice is exclusively devoted to criminal law. He does not split his focus across divorce, personal injury, real estate, or business disputes. When you hire Mr. Byrd, every ounce of his expertise is directed toward protecting your freedom and your future.

Practice Philosophy

Defending the Wrongly Accused for Over 40 Years

James M. Byrd built his career on a simple but powerful conviction: every person accused of a crime deserves the best possible defense, regardless of the charge. He comes from a family of criminal defense lawyers and was drawn to this work long before he passed the bar. For Mr. Byrd, defending the accused is not just a job. It is a calling.

When you are facing criminal charges, you are up against a well-funded prosecution with investigators, forensic resources, and experience. Mr. Byrd levels the playing field. He scrutinizes every piece of evidence, challenges every procedural misstep, and holds the government to the strict standards the Constitution demands. He has done this work continuously since 1979, and that unbroken dedication shows in every case he handles.

Mr. Byrd believes in direct, honest communication with his clients. He will explain your situation clearly, outline your options without exaggeration, and fight relentlessly for the outcome you deserve. When you hire James M. Byrd, you get James M. Byrd, not a paralegal or a junior associate.
