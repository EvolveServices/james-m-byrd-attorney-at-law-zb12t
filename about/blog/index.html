DUI Defense Strategies in Alabama

A DUI arrest in Alabama can result in license suspension, heavy fines, mandatory alcohol treatment programs, and even jail time. However, a DUI charge does not automatically mean a conviction. There are often viable defenses based on the circumstances of the traffic stop, the accuracy of breath or field sobriety tests, and the procedures followed by law enforcement during the arrest.
