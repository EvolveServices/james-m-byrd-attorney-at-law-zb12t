Attorney Biography

James M. Byrd

Attorney at Law • Mobile, Alabama • Criminal Defense Since 1979

James M. Byrd is a lifelong Mobile, Alabama resident and one of the most experienced criminal defense attorneys in the state. Born and raised in Mobile, Mr. Byrd graduated from Jefferson Davis High School in Montgomery before pursuing higher education at Duke University. He earned his undergraduate degree in Political Science and History from The University of Alabama, where he developed the analytical foundation that would define his legal career.

Criminal defense is not simply a profession for James Byrd. It is a calling rooted in family tradition. He comes from a family of criminal defense lawyers, and from an early age he understood the profound responsibility that criminal defense attorneys carry. Following his passion, he enrolled at The University of Alabama School of Law, where he prepared rigorously for the career ahead.

In 1979, Mr. Byrd earned his Juris Doctor and passed the Alabama State Bar. He moved immediately to Mobile and began practicing criminal defense alongside experienced attorneys who mentored and shaped his approach in court. Since October 1979, he has continued that practice every single day, never wavering from his commitment to criminal defense as his sole area of practice.

Over more than four decades, James M. Byrd has handled the full range of criminal matters: state felonies and misdemeanors, complex federal cases, white collar crime, DUI defense, drug offenses, and everything in between. His singular focus on criminal law means clients receive representation from an attorney whose entire career has been spent mastering this specific discipline. He does not split his focus across divorce, personal injury, real estate, or business disputes. When you hire Mr. Byrd, every ounce of his expertise is directed toward protecting your freedom and your future.

Practice Philosophy

Defending the Wrongly Accused for Over 40 Years

James M. Byrd built his career on a simple but powerful conviction: every person accused of a crime deserves the best possible defense, regardless of the charge. He comes from a family of criminal defense lawyers and was drawn to this work long before he passed the bar. For Mr. Byrd, defending the accused is not just a job. It is a calling.

When you are facing criminal charges, you are up against a well-funded prosecution with investigators, forensic resources, and experience. Mr. Byrd levels the playing field. He scrutinizes every piece of evidence, challenges every procedural misstep, and holds the government to the strict standards the Constitution demands.

Mr. Byrd believes in direct, honest communication with his clients. He will explain your situation clearly, outline your options without exaggeration, and fight relentlessly for the outcome you deserve. When you hire James M. Byrd, you get James M. Byrd, not a paralegal or a junior associate.
