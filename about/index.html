Attorney Biography

A Career Built on Criminal Defense

James M. Byrd was born and raised in Mobile, Alabama, and has devoted his entire legal career to defending the criminally accused in the courts of this state and nation. His roots run deep in Mobile, and so does his commitment to the people who live here.

After graduating from Jefferson Davis High School in Montgomery, Mr. Byrd pursued higher education at Duke University before earning his Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science and History from The University of Alabama. His academic foundation gave him a disciplined understanding of government, law, and the institutions that shape our justice system.

Criminal defense is not simply a profession for James Byrd. It is a calling rooted in family tradition. He comes from a family of criminal defense lawyers, and from an early age he understood the profound responsibility that criminal defense attorneys carry. Following his passion, he enrolled at The University of Alabama School of Law, where he prepared rigorously for the career ahead.

In 1979, Mr. Byrd graduated from law school and passed the Alabama State Bar. He moved immediately to Mobile and began practicing criminal defense alongside experienced attorneys who mentored and shaped his approach in court. Since October 1979, he has continued that practice every single day, never wavering from his commitment to criminal defense as his sole area of practice.

Over more than four decades, James M. Byrd has handled the full range of criminal matters: state felonies and misdemeanors, complex federal cases, white collar crime, DUI defense, drug offenses, and everything in between. His singular focus on criminal law means clients receive representation from an attorney whose entire career has been spent mastering this specific discipline.
