Courts Serving Washington County

Washington County Circuit Court (1st Judicial Circuit)

The 1st Judicial Circuit handles all felony criminal cases in Washington County, including drug offenses, violent crimes, property felonies, and DUI charges with prior convictions or aggravating factors.

Washington County District Court

Handles misdemeanor criminal cases, traffic violations, and preliminary hearings for felony matters before cases proceed to Circuit Court.

Chatom Municipal Court

Chatom is the Washington County seat and its municipal court handles city ordinance violations and lower-level misdemeanor offenses occurring within Chatom city limits.

McIntosh Municipal Court

Handles city ordinance violations and misdemeanor traffic matters for residents and visitors within McIntosh city limits in northern Washington County.
