Courts Serving Choctaw County

Choctaw County Circuit Court (1st Judicial Circuit)

The 1st Judicial Circuit handles all felony criminal cases in Choctaw County, including serious drug offenses, violent crimes, property felonies, and aggravated DUI charges.

Choctaw County District Court

Handles misdemeanor criminal matters, traffic violations, and preliminary hearings for felonies before cases are bound over to Circuit Court.

Butler Municipal Court

Butler is the county seat and its municipal court handles city ordinance violations and lower-level misdemeanor offenses occurring within Butler city limits.

Lisman Municipal Court

Handles city ordinance violations and misdemeanor traffic matters for residents and visitors within the Lisman city limits.
