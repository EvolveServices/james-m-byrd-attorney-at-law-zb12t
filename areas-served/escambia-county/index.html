Courts Serving Escambia County

Escambia County Circuit Court (21st Judicial Circuit)

The 21st Judicial Circuit handles all felony criminal matters in Escambia County, including drug trafficking, robbery, assault, sexual offenses, and DUI cases with prior convictions or aggravating circumstances.

Escambia County District Court

Handles misdemeanor criminal cases, traffic violations, small claims, and preliminary hearings for felony matters before they are transferred to Circuit Court.

Brewton Municipal Court

The Brewton Municipal Court handles city ordinance violations, lower-level traffic matters, and misdemeanor offenses occurring within the Brewton city limits.

Atmore Municipal Court

Atmore's municipal court handles local ordinance violations and misdemeanor traffic matters arising within the city of Atmore, the second-largest city in Escambia County.
