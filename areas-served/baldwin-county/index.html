Courts Serving Baldwin County

Baldwin County Circuit Court (28th Judicial Circuit)

The 28th Judicial Circuit handles all felony criminal matters in Baldwin County, including drug trafficking, robbery, assault, and serious DUI cases with prior offenses.

Baldwin County District Court

Handles misdemeanor criminal cases, traffic violations, and conducts preliminary hearings for felony cases before they are transferred to Circuit Court.

Bay Minette Municipal Court

Handles ordinance violations and lower-level misdemeanor offenses within the Bay Minette city limits.

Daphne, Fairhope, and Gulf Shores Municipal Courts

These active municipal courts in Baldwin County's growing cities handle DUI cases, ordinance violations, and misdemeanor charges within their respective jurisdictions.
