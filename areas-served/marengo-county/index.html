Courts Serving Marengo County

Marengo County Circuit Court (4th Judicial Circuit)

The 4th Judicial Circuit handles all felony criminal cases in Marengo County, including drug trafficking, violent crimes, property offenses, and serious DUI charges with prior convictions.

Marengo County District Court

Handles misdemeanor criminal matters, traffic violations, and preliminary hearings for felony cases before transfer to Circuit Court.

Linden Municipal Court

Linden, the county seat of Marengo County, has a municipal court that handles city ordinance violations and lower-level misdemeanor matters within Linden city limits.

Demopolis Municipal Court

Demopolis is Marengo County's largest city and its municipal court handles local ordinance violations, traffic matters, and misdemeanor offenses within the city.
