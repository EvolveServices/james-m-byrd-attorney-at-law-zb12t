Federal Jurisdiction

Federal Court Admissions

In addition to Alabama state courts, James M. Byrd is licensed to appear in federal courts at three levels of the federal judiciary, providing comprehensive representation for clients facing federal criminal charges.

U.S. District Court, Southern District of Alabama

Federal trial court handling criminal charges, including white collar crime, drug offenses, firearms violations, and other federal felonies prosecuted in Mobile and the surrounding region.

Eleventh Circuit Court of Appeals

The federal appellate court with jurisdiction over Alabama, Georgia, and Florida. Mr. Byrd is admitted to argue appeals of federal criminal convictions before this court in Atlanta.

The United States Supreme Court

Admission to practice before the highest court in the nation reflects the depth of Mr. Byrd's legal credentials and his commitment to pursuing every available avenue of relief for his clients.
