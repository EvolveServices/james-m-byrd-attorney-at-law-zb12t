Courts Serving Mobile County

Mobile County Circuit Court (13th Judicial Circuit)

Alabama's busiest circuit court, handling all felony criminal cases in Mobile County, including murder, assault, drug trafficking, and white collar crimes.

Mobile County District Court

Handles misdemeanors, traffic violations, preliminary hearings for felony cases, and small claims matters in Mobile County.

City of Mobile Municipal Court

Adjudicates violations of City of Mobile ordinances and lower-level misdemeanor offenses within the city limits.

Prichard and Saraland Municipal Courts

Municipal courts for the cities of Prichard and Saraland handle city ordinance violations and misdemeanor traffic matters within their jurisdictions.
