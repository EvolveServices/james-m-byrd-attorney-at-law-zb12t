Courts Serving Clarke County

Clarke County Circuit Court (1st Judicial Circuit)

The 1st Judicial Circuit handles all felony criminal matters in Clarke County, including drug trafficking, violent crimes, property crimes, and DUI cases with aggravating factors or prior convictions.

Clarke County District Court

Handles misdemeanor criminal cases, traffic violations, small claims, and preliminary hearings for felony charges before they are bound over to Circuit Court.

Grove Hill Municipal Court

The municipal court for Clarke County's seat handles city ordinance violations and lower-level misdemeanor offenses occurring within the city limits of Grove Hill.

Jackson Municipal Court

Jackson, the county's largest city, has its own municipal court handling local ordinance violations, traffic matters, and misdemeanor offenses arising within Jackson city limits.
