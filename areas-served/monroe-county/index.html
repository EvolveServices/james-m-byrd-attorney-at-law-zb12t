Courts Serving Monroe County

Monroe County Circuit Court (4th Judicial Circuit)

The 4th Judicial Circuit handles all felony criminal matters in Monroe County, including drug offenses, violent crimes, property felonies, and aggravated DUI charges with prior convictions.

Monroe County District Court

Handles misdemeanor criminal matters, traffic violations, and preliminary hearings for felony charges before they are bound over to Circuit Court.

Monroeville Municipal Court

The Monroeville Municipal Court handles city ordinance violations and misdemeanor matters occurring within the city limits of Monroeville, the Monroe County seat.

Evergreen Municipal Court

Handles local ordinance violations and misdemeanor matters within the city of Evergreen, located in neighboring Conecuh County but serving the broader South Alabama region.
