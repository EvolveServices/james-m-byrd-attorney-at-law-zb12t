Accessibility on jamesmbyrdlaw.com

James M. Byrd, Attorney at Law makes UserWay's Web Accessibility Widget available, which is powered by a dedicated accessibility server. The software allows jamesmbyrdlaw.com to improve its compliance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1).

Enabling the Accessibility Menu

The accessibility menu can be enabled by clicking the accessibility menu icon that appears on the page. After triggering the accessibility menu, please wait a moment for the accessibility menu to load in its entirety.

On Desktop or Laptop: The accessibility menu icon appears on the right side of the page.

On Mobile or Tablet: The accessibility menu icon appears at the bottom-right side of the page.

Once the menu is open, you can adjust text size, contrast, cursor size, and many other display and navigation settings to suit your individual needs. These preferences are saved for future visits to our site.

Our Commitment to Digital Accessibility

James M. Byrd, Attorney at Law is committed to making our digital presence as inclusive as possible. We believe that all individuals, regardless of ability, should have equal access to information about our legal services and the ability to contact our office without barriers.

We are continually working to improve the user experience for everyone who visits our site, applying relevant accessibility standards and guidelines wherever possible. Our commitment includes:

  • Regular review of website content and structure for accessibility compliance
  • Use of the UserWay accessibility widget to provide real-time accommodations
  • Ensuring adequate color contrast across all pages
  • Providing descriptive alt text for all meaningful images
  • Maintaining a logical heading structure for screen reader navigation
  • Ensuring all interactive elements are keyboard-navigable

Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is not a one-time project but an ongoing commitment. We regularly audit our website to identify areas for improvement and implement updates as technology and standards evolve. We monitor updates to WCAG guidelines and adjust our practices accordingly.

If you encounter any content or functionality that is not fully accessible to you, we want to know. Your feedback directly informs our improvement efforts and helps us serve all users more effectively.
