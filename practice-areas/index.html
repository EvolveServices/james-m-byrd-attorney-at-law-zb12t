DUI / DWI Defense

A DUI or DWI conviction in Alabama carries severe penalties including license suspension, mandatory fines, ignition interlock requirements, increased insurance premiums, and possible jail time. A first offense can derail your career, and subsequent offenses carry increasingly harsh consequences under Alabama law. Acting quickly after an arrest is critical, because there are strict deadlines to request an administrative license hearing and preserve your driving privileges.

James M. Byrd has defended DUI cases in Alabama for over 40 years. He knows how to scrutinize every element of a DUI stop: the legality of the traffic stop, the administration of field sobriety tests, the calibration and maintenance records of breathalyzer devices, and the handling of blood or urine samples. A strong DUI defense often hinges on procedural errors or constitutional violations that an experienced attorney can identify and exploit.

  • First offense, second offense, and felony DUI defense in Alabama
  • Challenging breathalyzer results, field sobriety tests, and blood draws
  • Administrative license hearing representation to protect driving privileges
  • DUI involving drugs, prescription medications, and commercial vehicles
